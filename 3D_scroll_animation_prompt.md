# 3D Scroll Animation Prompt

Create a 120-frame sequence where the floating rose water bottle performs a scroll-friendly mechanical **“expand → open → reassemble”** action. The bottle contains softly glowing blush-pink rose water with delicate Damascus rose petals suspended inside.

## Frame Breakdown

### Frames 0–20
Bottle fully assembled, centered, and stable in the air.

### Frames 21–55
Bottle subtly expands outward (precision widening and spacing between the silver cap and the glass body).

### Frames 56–85
Bottle opens into clean layers:
- Glass casing  
- Pink liquid core  
- Suspended petals  

All elements remain aligned with clean separations.

### Frames 86–119
Layers glide back together and the bottle returns to a fully assembled state.

## Style Notes
- Motion should feel **magnetic, engineered, and controlled**
- Use **gentle easing** throughout
- Maintain consistency in:
  - Camera angle  
  - Moody charcoal-grey background  
  - Water splash elements
